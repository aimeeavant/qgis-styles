Play-Eat-Walk Local!

Authored by: Aimee Armour-Avant

The Institute of Environmental Recreation has launched a campaign to promote an active lifestyle in city neighborhoods by showcasing maps that hightlight trails and amenities in city's neighborhoods that are within walking distance (one mile) of public parks, forests, and beaches.

This map shows a one-mile mile buffer around the public park Laguna Lake in San Luis Obispo County, California and selected food-based ammenities include including cafes, restaurants, ice cream, fast food, and bars.

Laguna Lake Park features several unique recreation areas and opportunities. Laguna Lake is a migratory stop and home to a variety of waterfowl.
